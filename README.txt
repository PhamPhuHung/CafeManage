CafeManage
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
An desktop application which help you to run your food-drink store more conveniently, including:
 - A food-drink menu that you can choose category
 - A table list where you can see your current tables status
 - A checkout where you can create bill for your customer
 - An user account management where you can manage your staff
 - A customer management where you can insert customer info & give them discount base on their bills
 - A food-drink warehouse management where you can manage your material input
 - An administration area where you can create-read-edit-delete your food-staff-customer-material-bill-profit-...
